03

How we work

Predictable, transparent, and bias-toward-shipping. Four phases, no surprises.

  1. /01

    Discovery

    One week. We map the problem, audit existing systems, and produce a written technical proposal with scope, timeline, and a fixed price.

  2. /02

    Design

    Two to three weeks. Wireframes become prototypes. Engineers join early so what we draw is what we can actually build.

  3. /03

    Build

    Iterative two-week sprints. You see working software at the end of each one, in a real staging environment.

  4. /04

    Ship & support

    Production launch with monitoring, on-call coverage for the first 30 days, and a structured handover or ongoing retainer.

04

A small team, on purpose.

Codegineers is built around a simple bet: senior people, working on fewer things at once, ship better software than large teams stretched thin.

Every project is staffed by people who have been doing this for a decade or more. No bench-warmers, no junior-led builds, no “we'll figure out who owns it later.” You get the same team from kickoff to launch, and we tell you exactly who they are before you sign anything.

We work with founders, product teams, and CTOs who care about craft and don't have patience for theatre. If that sounds like you, let's talk.
