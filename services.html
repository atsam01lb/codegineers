002 / Mobile apps

Apps that respect
the device.

Mobile is its own discipline. Touch targets, offline behaviour, battery life, push notifications, permissions, app store reviews — none of it is the web with different CSS. We build iOS and Android applications that feel native because they behave native.

Cross-platform with React Native or Flutter when the trade-offs make sense; fully native Swift or Kotlin when they don't. Either way, we'll be honest with you about which path fits your product.
